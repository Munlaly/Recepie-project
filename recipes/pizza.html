Pizza Recipe

Description

Even a novice cook can master the art of perfect pizza with our step-by-step guide. This homemade pizza recipe features a simple pizza dough and toppings.

List of ingredients

  • 300 g flour
  • 1 tsp instant yeap
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 1 ml passata
  • handful frash basil / 1 tsp dried
  • 1 crushed garlic cove
  • 125 g ball mozzarella
  • handful grated parmezan
  • handful of cherry tomatoes
  • handful of basil leaves

Method

  1. Step

    Make the base: Put the flour into a large bowl, then stir in the yeast and salt.

    Make a well, pour in 200ml warm water and the olive oil and bring together with

    a wooden spoon until you have a soft, fairly wet dough. Turn onto a lightly floured surface

    and knead for 5 mins until smooth. Cover with a tea towel and set aside.

    You can leave the dough to rise if you like, but it’s not essential for a thin crust.

  2. Step

    Make the sauce: Mix the passata, basil and crushed garlic together,

    then season to taste. Leave to stand at room temperature while you get on with shaping the base.

  3. Step

    Roll out the dough: if you’ve let the dough rise, give it a quick knead,

    then split into two balls. On a floured surface, roll out the dough into large rounds,

    about 25cm across, using a rolling pin. The dough needs to be very thin as it will rise

    in the oven. Lift the rounds onto two floured baking sheets.

  4. Step

    Top and bake: heat the oven to 240C/220C fan/gas 8. Put another baking sheet

    or an upturned baking tray in the oven on the top shelf. Smooth sauce over bases with the back of a spoon.

    Scatter with cheese and tomatoes, drizzle with olive oil and season. Put one pizza, still on its baking sheet,

    on top of the preheated sheet or tray. Bake for 8-10 mins until crisp. Serve with a little more olive oil, and basil

    leaves if using. Repeat step for remaining pizza.
